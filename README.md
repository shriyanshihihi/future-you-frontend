## Inspiration

During moments of uncertainty, self-doubt, or pressure, many of us wish we could hear reassurance from a wiser version of ourselves ‚Äî someone who has already lived through the confusion and come out stronger.

**Dear Me** was inspired by a simple question:

> _What if your future self could write you a letter today?_

This project uses AI not for automation or productivity, but for **reflection, comfort, and emotional clarity**.

---

## What This Project Does

**Dear Me** is a web application that allows users to:
1. Reflect on their present struggles, fears, and dreams
2. Choose a future year
3. Receive a personalized letter written by their **future self**

The result feels intimate, human, and reassuring ‚Äî like a message from someone who truly understands you.

---

## User Flow

1. **Reflection Form**  
   Users answer guided prompts about:
   - Current struggles
   - Dreams they‚Äôre afraid to admit
   - How they hope life feels in the future

2. **Writing Experience**  
   A dedicated ‚Äúwriting‚Äù page builds anticipation while the AI crafts the letter.

3. **Final Letter**  
   The letter:
   - Types out gradually for emotional effect
   - Appears as a postcard
   - Can be downloaded, copied, or shared
